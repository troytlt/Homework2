About Me

Hello World!

I love working out, hanging out with interesting people and always stay confident in whatever I do. I love solving problems and widening my own horizons. This is probably the reason why I love coding.

I came to learn coding a little late in life. But that's not the reason why I shall learn it slowlier than anybody else. Coding is interesting and requires a thorough mindset. I believe if one does not fully get commited to coding, then he shall never see the silver light at the end of the tunnel. Though it is daunting and challenging at times, I feel motivated to put more efforts into solving any problems occuring, which makes me grow and explore my potential.

I have various goals. And the goal I must accomplish before I turn 30 would be to become a global citizen, meaning I can go anywhere in the world without having to change my career. For that, coding would grant me this wish and I believe the opportunities in the coding field would be endless.
